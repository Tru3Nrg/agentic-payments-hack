# Add Your Game Item Images Here

To add the sword and shield images:

1. **Place your image files in this directory** (`public/images/game-items/`):
   - `sword.png` (or `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.webp`)
   - `shield.png` (or `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.webp`)

2. **Supported formats**: PNG, JPG, JPEG, WebP

3. **Recommended size**: 400x400px or larger (will be automatically optimized)

4. **File names must match exactly**:
   - `sword.png` for the Sword of Monad
   - `shield.png` for the Shield of Gasless

Once you add the images, they will automatically appear in the game store at `/game-store`.

**Note**: If you have images with different names or formats, you can update the paths in `lib/store/items.ts` to match your file names.
